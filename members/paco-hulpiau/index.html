Paco Hulpiau

Paco Hulpiau technician

In 1999 I graduated in Farmaceutical and Biological Techniques at the University College West Flanders (Howest) in Bruges.

From 1999 to 2006 I worked as a laboratory technician in the Laboratory of Protein Biochemistry and Protein Engineering at Ghent University. During these years I studied informatics with a focus on programming and obtained a teaching degree.

In 2006 I joined the bioinformatics core facility at VIB in Ghent and, also after the integration of our core facility in the DaMBi research group, I am responsible for providing bioinformatics services for the research groups in the Inflammation Research Center (IRC).

Yearly I teach the structural bioinformatics course at Howest as a guest lecturer.
